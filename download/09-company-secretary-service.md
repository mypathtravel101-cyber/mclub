## Why Company Secretary Service Matters

In Hong Kong's regulatory environment, every limited company is **legally required** to appoint a Company Secretary within 6 months of incorporation. Non-compliance can result in severe penalties and prosecution. Beyond compliance, a professional company secretary serves as the backbone of corporate governance — ensuring your business runs smoothly, stays compliant, and is positioned for growth.

For cross-border entrepreneurs and family offices under MCLUB, the company secretary role extends far beyond filing documents. It is the **critical interface** between your wealth structure and Hong Kong's legal, tax, and regulatory framework. Damon Lewis provides this essential service with the backing of MCLUB's legal expertise and global network.

## Four Core Service Pillars

### Pillar 1: 會計與稅務 (Accounting & Tax)

**Professional accounting and tax compliance services** — the financial foundation of every successful business.

**Key Services:**

- Preparation of financial statements (財務報表編製)

### Pillar 2: 公司成立與合規 (Formation & Compliance)

**End-to-end company incorporation and statutory compliance** — ensuring your business structure is legally sound from day one.

**Key Services:**
- **會計及公司秘書 (Accounting & Company Secretary)**
  - Statutory company secretary appointment (法定公司秘書委任)
  - Maintenance of statutory books and registers (法定簿冊及登記冊維護)
  - Preparation and filing of Annual Return (周年申報表)
  - Changes in directors, shareholders, registered address (董事、股東、註冊地址變更)
  - Minutes and resolutions preparation (會議紀錄及決議編製)

- **稅務規則 (Tax Rules & Regulations)**
  - Hong Kong Inland Revenue Department compliance (香港稅務局合規)
  - Employer's Return of Remuneration and Pensions (僱主報稅表)
  - Transfer pricing documentation and compliance (轉讓定價文檔及合規)

- **周年申報 (Annual Filing)**
  - Annual Return filing with Companies Registry (公司註冊處周年申報)
  - Business Registration Certificate renewal (商業登記證續期)
  - Dormant company compliance management (不活動公司合規管理)

**Why It Matters:**
Hong Kong's Companies Ordinance imposes strict deadlines for annual filings and statutory updates. Missing a filing deadline can result in prosecution, fines, and reputational damage. Damon Lewis ensures every deadline is met, every register is updated, and your company maintains good standing — the essential prerequisite for banking, investment, and cross-border transactions.

### Pillar 4: 中小企融資顧問 (SME Financing Advisor)

**Professional loan facilitation for SMEs** — securing business capital without collateral through Hong Kong's government-backed lending schemes.

- SME Financing Guarantee Scheme application (中小企融資擔保計劃)
- No collateral required — backed by HKMC Insurance Limited (香港按揭證券公司)
- **Up to 89% guarantee coverage** — significantly reducing bank risk and increasing approval rates
- **Fast approval process** — expedited application and bank negotiation
- **Maximum loan amount: HK$18,000,000**
- Working capital financing (營運資金融資)
- Term loan restructuring and optimization

**Why It Matters:**
Cash flow is the lifeblood of every business. Many viable SMEs struggle to secure bank financing due to collateral requirements. Damon Lewis leverages the Hong Kong government's SME Financing Guarantee Scheme to unlock capital without pledging assets — turning your business potential into real, deployable capital. With up to HK$18,000,000 available, this can be transformative for expansion, equipment acquisition, or bridging seasonal cash flow gaps.
